Stephen Sheldon is a Florida based photographer. Stephen seeks to explore the world through his camera in order to capture people and the environments they interact with. Having worked and traveled to many different countries, Stephen understands the nuances of different cultures which aides him with his international photography. His work focuses on photojournalism, commercial, street and travel photography.
